about me

My name is Jorge, a London based music producer, sound designer and creative programmer.

I am currently studying creative computing at Goldsmiths, University of London.

My work is heavily focused around sound design and complex textures. I love making everything from scratch, including my own plugins using Max for Live.

In my spare time I like noodling around on my guitar and running it through my modular synth, playing with TouchDesigner, and looking for old tape recorders to add to my studio collection.
